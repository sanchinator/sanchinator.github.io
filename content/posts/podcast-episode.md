## Why this topic?

Calin and I talk about AI a lot between ourselves, Claude specifically. We wanted to bring in someone who uses these tools professionally to see whether our take held up against someone using them on real projects. The security angle mattered: most people adopt AI tools fast and think about the risks later, and we wanted to push on that.

## Summary points

- The gap between junior and senior engineers doesn't disappear with AI, it shifts. Knowing when the output is wrong matters more than ever.
- MCPs and agents are still mostly experiments in the industry, but the direction is clear.
- Security is a genuine concern: what you give an AI tool access to deserves as much thought as what you get back from it.
